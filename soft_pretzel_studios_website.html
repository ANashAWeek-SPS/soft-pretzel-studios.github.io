About Me

My name is Nashiem, and I am the Founder of Soft Pretzel Studios, based in Richmond, VA. Currently, I am focusing on a mobile game, but I plan for most of my games to be released on Steam. Along with game development, I run a YouTube channel where I discuss games I like, indie games, and create video essays. I am a solo developer using tools like Godot 4, GitHub, Audacity, and Krita.
