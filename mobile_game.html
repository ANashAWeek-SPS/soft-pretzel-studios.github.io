Project Zero Dawn

About the Project

Project Zero Dawn is an exciting 2D cartoony, fast-paced autorunner game where you evade the clutches of your worst nightmare... the government! The IRS is hot on your heels, and you must run, jump, and dash to stay out of jail!

Game Features

  • Genre: 2D Cartoony Fast-Paced Platformer
  • Platform: Initially for mobile (Android and iOS), with plans for a Steam release.
  • Core Mechanics: Auto-runner with simple tap controls for jumping, long jumps, and wall jumps.
  • Unique Levels: Designed for short bursts of gameplay (1-2 minutes per level) to ensure fun and varied experiences.
  • Themes: Light-hearted and goofy, centered around the humorous dangers of tax evasion.
  • Character: Play as Mr. Moneybags, a clever and goofy family man on the run from Mr. IRS.

Inspirations

  • Pizza Tower
  • Vector
  • Super Mario Run
  • Geometry Dash

Target Audience

Designed for players who enjoy quick, humorous gameplay sessions on their mobile devices. Ideal for fans of light-hearted games with a quirky twist.

Development Tools

  • Game Engine: Godot 4.1.1
  • Art: Krita
  • Sound: Audacity

Progression

Players will progress through levels by outpacing the IRS, earning in-game currency to purchase fun cosmetics.

Future Plans

Post-launch plans include a "Silver Edition" for Steam with expanded levels, more characters, and new modes.

Stay tuned for updates and more information on the development of Project Zero Dawn!
